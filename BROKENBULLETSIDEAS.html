Broken Bullets: Arise

Danny’s wife is killed by terrorists, so Danny goes on a rampage to kill all of the terrorists.

--In terrorist leader’s neighborhood--

Danny has run in with law enforcement, so he runs away then holds off the cops while he

calls a helicopter.

--The deserts of india--

Danny is then shot down by terrorists when flying over the desert.

--TITLE CARD--

Danny tries to save the friend in the helicopter, but there is only one parachute. Danny grieves his death

--A large array of terrorist camps--

A few terrorists come to see if Danny is dead, but Danny kills them.

Danny has the option to explore the camps, siege them, continue the story etc.

(Open world)

--Meeting room--

Danny finds the terrorist leader (Aarav) having an arms deal, so he throws a grenade and runs.

--Edge of desert--

Everyone follows Danny and tries to kill him.

Danny is shot and can’t walk

While on his last breath, his helicopter friend drives an armored truck over the terrorists and invites Danny on.

--Passing the border--

Border police stop and try to arrest Danny, but Danny runs away. Swat teams are now on his trail, and Danny remembers his wife, and that she wouldn’t like him that way, so he turns himself in.

--Trial--

He is sentenced to 3 years in prison and so is his friend.

--END SCENE--

[3 Years later]

Him and his friend are walking out of the prison grounds.His friend gives his wife a hug, and the character grips his necklace. Danny is then being escorted by helicopter

Game 2:

Broken Bullets: Dynasty

Premise:
--In the skies of Nepal--

Danny notices Aarav having conversations with Hunter and tells the pilot to stop the helicopter, Helicopter pilot refuses so Danny has to jump out. Danny ends up not making it all the way and pulling his parachute too slow and injures himself.

--The middle of nepal--

Since Danny doesn’t know where he is he wanders around until he comes across a shrine. Danny says hi to the worshippers who welcome him. One of the Hindus noticed his arm and decided to treat it. Danny is thankful at first but then hesitant when he sees the tools they use. They feed him a sedative poison and proceed to cut off his dislocated joint. They use vines to wrap his wound, and proceed to wake him up. Danny starts screaming, but then thanks them after realizing that it didn’t hurt as much as before. Danny leaves the shrine and walks towards the mountains.

--The cities of nepal--

Everyone looks at Danny like he’s insane. When he goes to see the doctor, the doctor says that Danny has a rare infection that doesn’t have treatment. The doctor tells Danny to probably use a prosthetic, then tells him someone in town that knows how to do that.

--Vishnu Patel’s shop--

Danny goes to Vishnu’s shop, and Vishnu has seen him on the news, and says he’d like to help. He says that since he wants to support Danny he can make custom prosthetics. Vishnu comes out of the corner with a flashlight on a prosthetic hand, which alarms Danny. Vishnu comes back with a sword on another one, and tells Danny he can do that with pretty much any object.

--TITLE CARD--

Vishnu sends Danny and tells him that there’s a base in the mountains where Aarav’s partner in crime is hiding.

--Lower mountains--

While walking up the mountains Danny hears an announcement over the city of a masked figure killing someone in a chair with a bag over their head, then states that there’s more to come if the citizens aren’t willing to pay up
